About us

Precision in the diagnosis of diverse central nervous system (CNS) tumour types is critical for optimal treatment.

I-DIMENSIONS is a classification system subtyping Diffuse Midline glioma (DMG) from DNA methylation profiles, whole genome sequence (WGS) and H&E imaging.



Why it matters

Accurate pathological diagnosis plays an important role in management of patients with DMG. Previous related projects with low accuracy limited in Uni-modality analysis such as genomic-led project ZERO. There is a pressing need to accurate, robust and confident classify DMG subtypes. In this project, we plan to build up the artificial intelligence (AI) based subtyping system to accurately classify DMG subtypes from multi-omics data (DNA methylation, WGS and H&E imaging). This comprehensive approach will provide well-structured clinical decision-making process and offer objective and data-driven decision support. We expect that the principle of using multi-omics signatures as part of a tumour subtyping will improve diagnostic accuracy not only in DMG but will also with the potential to be served as a blueprint in other brain tumour diagnostics.
